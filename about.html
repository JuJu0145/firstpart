About Us

Founded in 2018, EduHope Foundation is dedicated to improving educational outcomes for children in under-resourced communities. We believe education is the cornerstone of a better future.

Our Vision

To see every child in South Africa have equal access to quality education and opportunities.

Our Mission

We aim to empower children through tutoring, mentorship, and resource provision, supported by dedicated volunteers and donors.
